About Us

History, Mission & Vision

Founded in 2016, the University Ciputra Debate Society (UCDS) started as a small group of enthusiasts and grew into a thriving community of debaters, adjudicators, and coaches. Our purpose is to cultivate open-minded dialogue, rigorous analysis, and confident communication.

Critical Thinking
We challenge assumptions and evaluate evidence.
Teamwork

Inclusivity
Everyone is welcome—no experience required.

Programs

Activities & Training

Weekly Meetings

Every Wednesday, 18:00 at Room A201. Format rotates between BP drills, case files, and rebuttal labs.

Training Programs

Workshops, practice debates, and adjudication training for all levels— led by senior members and alumni coaches.

Special Leagues

Agora (beginners), Socratic (intermediate), and Aristotle (advanced). Progression-focused with feedback.

UCDC

UCDC is a debate competition that we direct every year, both international and national.

Partnerships

We collaborate with schools, universities, and debate societies for scrims, clinics, and shared tournaments.

FAQs for Newcomers

Do I need experience?

No. We welcome complete beginners and provide tiered leagues to help you progress.

Is there a membership fee?

We keep fees minimal and offer sponsorships when competing externally.

How often do we meet?

Weekly meetings on Thursdays, plus optional weekend workshops or scrims.
